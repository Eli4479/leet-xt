Extend your leetcode
  • Friends

Friends

1) Add Users to Friends Page from their Profile Page (using the star button).



2) Goto Friends Page from Anywhere (using the friends icon).



3) View Users on Friends Page (sortable).



4) Import / Export of Friends is supported (from extension options).



Contest Rating Predict

1) Predict Your Rating Before Official Contest Result (using the special button on contest page).



2) View Friends Participating and their Predicted Ratings (on past contests too).



Company Problems

1) View all Problems of Company (with duration + frequency).



2) View Locked Editorial of Problems.



Problem Rating + Companies

1) View Problem Rating + Problem Contest and Company Tags on Problems Page (using the locked companies button).
